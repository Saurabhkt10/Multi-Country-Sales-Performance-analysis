# multi-country sales performance analysis

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates a multi-country sales performance analysis developed using anonymized pharmaceutical sales data. The objective is to provide management with actionable insights through product, country, and category performance analysis.

---

## Objective

Develop a sales performance analysis model to evaluate revenue trends, product contribution, category performance, and country-wise sales distribution. The analysis supports management in monitoring business performance and making data-driven decisions.

---

## Data Description

- Period Covered: January 2023 – May 2026
- Products: 50 Anonymized SKUs
- Countries: Multiple African Markets
- Data Frequency: Monthly Sales Transactions
- Data Preparation: Power Query used to consolidate and transform multiple country datasets.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Sales
- Product Contribution
- Country Contribution
- Category Contribution
- Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth
- Product Performance Ranking

---

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Power Query
- Pivot Tables
- XLOOKUP
- INDEX/MATCH
- SUMIFS
- Power BI *(Dashboard under development)*

---

## Key Findings

- Identified top-performing products and countries.
- Measured product and country contribution to total sales.
- Performed Year-over-Year growth analysis.
- Generated executive insights to support business decision-making.

---

## Business Value

This analysis helps management to:

- Monitor sales performance.
- Identify growth opportunities.
- Evaluate product and market contribution.
- Support strategic business planning.
- Improve management reporting.
